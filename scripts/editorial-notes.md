# Popcorn Editorial Notes
# Updated as we curate together — used to improve scoring over time.
# Format: each entry = date, decision type (REMOVED / ADDED), article title, and reason.

## What Popcorn IS
- High-signal cultural moments that people are actively talking about
- Cross-domain collisions (sport × culture, AI × internet behaviour, etc.)
- Stories with genuine discourse, tension, or surprise
- Record-breaking moments with real audience scale

## What Popcorn is NOT
- General news or politics
- Celebrity gossip without cultural ripple
- Industry-only news (casting, production updates, trailers with no traction)
- Promotional content dressed as news
